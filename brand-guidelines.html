Identity Mark

Logo

The Tester.io mark embodies our commitment to excellence — a golden checkmark symbolizing certified quality, trust, and the highest standard in luxury jewelry.

Clear Space

1x minimum 1x min

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the checkmark mark around the logo at all times. This ensures the mark retains its visual impact and dignity.

Do Use
Use only approved logo files. Maintain proportions and clear space. Gradient version is preferred on dark surfaces.
Don't Distort
Never stretch, rotate, add effects, or change the logo colors. Do not place on busy or clashing backgrounds.
Minimum Size
The logo should never appear smaller than 32px in digital or 12mm in print to maintain legibility of the checkmark.

Stroke Weight
All icons use a consistent 1.2px stroke weight. This ensures visual harmony and a refined, delicate appearance at all sizes.
Color Usage
Icons should always use the brand gold (#C6A559) as the stroke color. On hover or active states, transition to #E6B979.
Sizing
Standard icon size is 36–40px within a 60px container. Scale proportionally and never below 24px to maintain clarity.

06

Verbal Identity

Brand Voice

The Tester.io voice is a quiet authority — confident without being loud, luxurious without being ostentatious. Every word is chosen with the same precision as every gemstone.

Refined Luxury

Speak to discernment, not ostentation. Our audience values quality that speaks for itself, not brands that shout.

"Where master artisans transform the world's rarest gems into heirloom pieces that transcend generations."

Timeless Authority

Write with the confidence of heritage and expertise. Our words carry weight because they are earned through craftsmanship.

"Every gemstone holds a universe of light, and every design should honor its brilliance."

Emotional Resonance

Connect pieces to life's most meaningful moments. Jewelry marks milestones — our words should honor that significance.

"Crafting extraordinary jewelry for life's most precious moments."

Elegant Simplicity

Use precise language. Remove excess. Every sentence should feel inevitable — nothing added, nothing missing.

"Timeless Elegance, Crafted to Perfection."
Do
  • Use evocative, sensory language that conjures imagery
  • Speak with quiet confidence and understated authority
  • Reference heritage, craft, and legacy
  • Address the customer as a discerning individual
  • Let the product's quality speak through description
Don't
  • Use aggressive sales language or urgency tactics
  • Over-explain or use excessive superlatives
  • Sound casual, trendy, or use slang
  • Make unsubstantiated claims about exclusivity
  • Use exclamation marks or all-caps emphasis
